# 👨‍💻 PhD Researcher in AI for Avionics Security

## 🚀 About Me
I am a PhD researcher at Polytechnique Montréal specializing in the intersection of **Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, and Avionics Systems**. My work focuses on developing intelligent and certifiable solutions for **GNSS security and aviation communication systems**, with applications in safety-critical environments.

My research aims to bridge the gap between **modern AI techniques** and **aviation certification requirements**, ensuring robustness, explainability, and operational reliability.
